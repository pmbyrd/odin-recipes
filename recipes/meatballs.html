Meatballs

Description

A simple meatball recipe.

This is a simple meatball recipe that is easy to make and tastes great. Can be served with pasta or rice. Cover with your favorite sauce or gravy. Great as an appetizer or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, egg, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Form the mixture into 1 inch balls.
  4. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
